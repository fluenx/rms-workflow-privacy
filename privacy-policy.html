Privacy Policy – RMS Workflow (Internal Chrome Extension)

1. Overview

RMS Workflow is a private internal Chrome extension developed exclusively for the HR team at Logicose. The extension is designed to streamline recruitment workflows, manage templates, and automate candidate communication. It is not intended for public use.

2. Data Access and Use

The extension may access and process the following data to provide its functionality:

  • Candidate information on internal RMS pages (e.g., name, email, phone number)
  • Website content required to populate templates and automate communication workflows

This data is accessed only within the user’s browser session and is used strictly for internal HR operations.

3. Data Storage

The extension uses Chrome’s local storage to store:

  • User preferences
  • Cached templates
  • Temporary workflow state needed for operation

No personal data is transmitted to external servers.

4. Data Sharing

The extension does not sell, rent, or share data with third parties. Data may be transmitted only to:

  • Logicose internal systems
  • Approved services required for functionality (e.g., Gmail, WhatsApp Web, GitHub for template sync)

All access is restricted to necessary domains only.

5. Security

The extension operates within Chrome’s security model and requests minimal permissions required for functionality. No tracking, analytics, or advertising scripts are used.

6. Third-Party Access

The extension does not collect passwords, payment information, health data, or location data.

7. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated to reflect changes in functionality or compliance requirements. Updates will apply only to internal users.
